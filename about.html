Efficiency, Authenticity, & Creativity

These are my core beliefs and I strive to produce work reflective of my values. I have tons of questions and spend my days trying to find answers to them, often blundering through many trials that lead to an error to get to the result I want. Now is the time for experimentation, to see what I am good at and what I like, and I built this website to see what I could do with web design. I write to get my thoughts on paper in order to reflect on all of the information and experiences I'm exposed to as a young adult out of college, on the exciting journey of self-discovery. I've always been a proponent of value maximization, trying to get the most out of everything. Read my thoughts, browse my guides, and hear from my experiences to learn more!
