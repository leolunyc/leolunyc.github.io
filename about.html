Let me tell you about me

Background

I grew up in a suburb in New Jersey and started developing a curiosity in learning from picking up various hobbies throughout middle school and high school. From learning how to skateboard to eventually making YouTube videos showcasing my self-taught guitar playing, I was never hesitant to jump into something new.

My journey began soon after that, when I began selling the electronics I taught myself how to fix on eBay and Craigslist. This sparked my interest in business. Moving to New York City, I went to college at NYU, worked internships in finance, and now continue to work in the field.

Along the way, I've stayed continuously curious, diving deep to explore and come up with answers. This site is a showcase of this never-ending process, to see life through the values of efficiency, authenticity, and creativity.
